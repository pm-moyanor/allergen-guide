Wheat

Starting your baby on wheat is an important milestone in their feeding journey. This guide will help you introduce wheat safely with alternating days - one day for feeding, one day for monitoring. Each feeding day gradually increases portions while rest days let you watch for any reactions.

Choose One Wheat Option

Iron-Rich Baby Cereal

Spoon-fed
  • Mix cereal with warm breast milk/formula
  • Thin, smooth consistency
  • Serve lukewarm
  • 1 Serving = 2 tablespoon

Tiny Pasta

BLW
  • Well-cooked until soft
  • Cool completely
  • Let baby self-feed
  • 1 Serving = 2 tablespoon

Cream of Wheat

Spoon-fed
  • Cook until very smooth
  • Mix with warm breastmilk/formula
  • Thin consistency
  • Serve lukewarm
  • 1 Serving = 2 tablespoon

Toast Fingers

BLW
  • 2" x 3" strips (adult finger size)
  • Lightly toasted
  • Let baby self-feed
  • 1 Serving = 1 strip
